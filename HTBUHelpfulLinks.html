Helpful Links for Unhealthy People

by

Cal Berkley

When searching for advice on how to develop a truly unhealthy lifestyle, great sources can be hard to find.

NO MORE!

I've compiled a list of amazing links that will provide you with tips, direction and inspiration for every facet of your new unhealthy life.

If you're venturing out into the brave new world of unhealthy living, these websites can be tremendous resources to point you in the right direction!

  • 10 Ways to Be Unhealthy and Miserable — This is a great resource if you're in a hurry and need to run through a checklist of the best unhealthy strategies!

  • 75 Unhealthiest Foods on the Planet — While this website tries to steer people AWAY from these foods, we know good food when we see it! Put all of these items on your grocery list!

  • Unhealthy Chain Restaurant Meals — When you absolutely must leave your house and STILL need to find a unhealthy meal, here is your list of where to go and what to order!

  • Paula Deen's Most Outrageous Recipes — From the Queen of unhealthy meals to your kitchen table! Paula is one of our Unhealthy Heroes, and this list shows why!

  • How to Dress Well as an Overweight Man — If you have no choice but to change out of your t-shirt and sweatpants, this site provides you with tips on how to show off your unhealthy build!

  • KingSize Direct — Speaking of dressing for your hefty build, this website is your one-stop shop!

  • La-Z-Boy Furniture — When striving to live an unhealthy lifestyle, there one place you should spend most of your time, and that's on a soft couch or in a comfortable recliner! Find all styles of lethargy-inducing furntiture here!

  • How to Waste Time — Once your fridge is stocked with junk food and your living room is full of the most comfy furniture, there's only one thing left to do—NOTHING! Find tips on how to waste your day right here!

With a list like that, you'll be on your way to complete lack of health in no time! From food to clothes to ways to do absolutely nothing, this is your go-to list!

We're always on the lookout for more unhealthy life hacks, so head on over to the Contact page and submit your own ideas!

— Cal Berkley is HTBU's resident Lifestyle Guru, offering helpful tips and life hacks for people pursuing poor health and style. Cal resides in Rough and Ready, California, with his unhappy wife and three overweight children.
